What it does

Batch video generation

Render dozens of short clips from source footage with consistent formatting, captions, and intros.

TikTok integration

Connect a TikTok creator account via official OAuth and upload approved clips as drafts for review.

Scheduling

Queue clips for publication at optimal times across multiple connected accounts.

Multi-platform

Publish the same short video to TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and VK Clips.

How TikTok integration works

The user signs in with their TikTok account through the official Login Kit (OAuth). Video Automation requests the user.info.basic scope to display the connected creator's profile in the dashboard, and the video.upload scope to upload pre-rendered MP4 files to the creator's TikTok account as drafts. The creator reviews and publishes each draft from the official TikTok app — Video Automation never publishes content without explicit creator approval.
