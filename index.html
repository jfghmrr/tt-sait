How TikTok integration works

  1. Click Sign in with TikTok on this page or on the /login page.
  2. You will be redirected to TikTok's official Login Kit and asked to grant the user.info.basic and video.upload scopes.
  3. After you approve, TikTok redirects you back to this site with an authorization code. The Video Automation desktop app receives that code and exchanges it for an access token using its Client Secret on your local machine.
  4. From the desktop app, you select pre-rendered MP4 files and the app uploads them to your TikTok account as drafts via the Content Posting API (video.upload). The drafts wait in your TikTok inbox until you review and publish them from the official TikTok app — Video Automation never publishes content without your explicit approval.
