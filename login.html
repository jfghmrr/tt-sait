Sign in with TikTok

Connect your TikTok creator account to Video Automation through the official TikTok Login Kit. We never see or store your TikTok password — authentication happens entirely on TikTok.

What permissions are requested

user.info.basic

Read your TikTok display name, avatar URL, open ID, and union ID — used to show which account is connected inside the Video Automation dashboard.

video.upload

Upload pre-rendered MP4 files you have explicitly approved to your TikTok account as drafts. You review and publish each draft from the official TikTok app — Video Automation never publishes content without your approval.

What happens after you sign in

  1. You are redirected to https://www.tiktok.com/v2/auth/authorize/ — the official TikTok consent screen.
  2. You confirm the requested scopes (user.info.basic and video.upload) on TikTok.
  3. TikTok redirects you back to https://sergeimironov.online with an authorization code and your original state parameter for CSRF protection.
  4. The Video Automation desktop app reads the code and exchanges it for an access token using its Client Secret — that exchange happens locally on your machine, never in the browser.

How to revoke access

You can revoke Video Automation's access at any time from your TikTok account settings: TikTok app → Settings and privacy → Security and login → Manage app permissions. You can also disconnect from inside the Video Automation desktop app.
